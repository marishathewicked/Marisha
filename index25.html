from space to Cherry


Take me home.
Take me home, the cold metal.
I am afraid that God will not look up.
No one is here to pray to in the darkness.
How many night have I laid wide awake,
Thoughts steal my dreams.
But even in one I saw an oscillating galactic call.
Otherwise I am dreaming here.
My fatigue steals from within me,
And takes me away to the grass.
I remember how I met you.
There was a cherry in your cocktail.
I was lost in your eyes deep as the eternal universe.

Take me to her, the cold metal.
Time after time a hundred suns explode in the galaxy.
Right now some race is dying in the depths of space.
And still, there was nothing as attractive, as being part of this intention.
Stars scribble in my eyes the memory about their unsafe lives.
They desire to be unmissed by someone,
Even if only in the shadow-realm

Take me home, the cold metal.
My Cherry, I'm beginning you to not make me choose between my sacred and you.
In outer space my cosmic soul was born.
It always wishes to drag me here.
Despite that my earth soul is yours.
Our attraction will never be equal to zero,
Even thought million light-years.
I will feel you, Cherry.

Take me to space, the cold metal.
