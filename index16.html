The Wings

He is able to dream about wings.
But they didn't allow him to keep these thoughts.
They never could appreciate his kindness.
They never will be able to understand him.
Be careful.They may betray you.

They must respect his otherness.
They may have stopped him.
But they were afraid of him.
They are trying to stop him now.
But they may be at risk.
He can fly now.

He doesn't need them anymore.
He might be tired of this fight.
But they will not be able to be the winners.
They have to beg for mercy.
They have to kneel in frount of him.
He can fly now.

He can cut the sky with his wings.
He didn't need to listen to their voices.
He needn't have been afraid.
His wings can carry him to the stars tonight.
