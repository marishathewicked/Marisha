The soul is over the stars


Ahead of clouds, over the countless roofs
From time to time brilliant flashes of light are playing
Steel wings are caring him to the East
Contributing to the excitement of the new experience

The soul is over the stars
Calm of distant worlds reflects in the eyes
Yesterday he was swimming with whales
Today, his throat will be burnt by hot air with taste of spices

He loves the sound of the ice crashing beneath the ship in Antarctica
He always throws a coin to a violinist on Spanish roads
He wins this life
He knows a thousand strangers stories

Over a cup of decent coffee,
At the foot of the great temple
He is reading the crackling pages of the guide
Tomorrow night he will walk slowly through the lamp-lighted streets of Rome

He takes his notes on the tattered book
Turning the stories having read by many to memories belonging to one
