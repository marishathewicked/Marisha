5575 it's not about sex


It is half past seven.
She is walking slowly down the stairs.
Her fingers are slipping on the wooden railing.
A sharp look in his eyes are holding her rooted to the ground.
Her heart is racing with anxiety.

The suit was made from Scabal.
The satin tie is wide.
A convulsive inhale and long exhale.
Knocking heels break and melt the ice in his double whiskey.
She is too close now.

They are standing facing each other.
Complete silence for a few seconds ... a lifetime.
The trivial questions are whispered.
Quiet words are flying around in an empty room.
He is sliding down on her cheek with the back of his hand,
On the neck, in the middle of the chest,
It turns right and enfolds her with embrace.

He pulled her hard and put her on his chest.
A smug smile is beaming on his face.
She is now his trophy!
She is ran her hands through his soft hair.
He is now her loot!
