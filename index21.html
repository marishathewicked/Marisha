The Crazy Night

I found an envelope in my postbox last Friday morning. Inside the envelope, there was an invitation for one to a private party called "Silver Flamingos." I would have to be at the specified address at midnight. I was hesitating to go for a long time. Eventually, I wore a plain black dress, black shoes with small heels and a small silver pendant in the form of the moon. With I found at the house of my grand-grandmother the last time. No one from my family knew where it came from. This pendant is so valuable to me as a connection to my ancestors.

I came by taxi to the appointed place at midnight. There was an unremarkable house. I knocked. The heavy door opened. I entered. At the doorstep, I met a short man in a suit with a pink tie. His eyes sparkled and he smiled broadly.

- Can I see your invitation? - he asked.

-Yes of course. - I answered.

He looked at the invitation and then he made a gesture.

- Welcome! - he added.

I took a few steps and saw something incredible. I found myself in the middle of a huge room. Large pink crystal chandelier hung overhead. The floor looked like a mirror. In it's reflection, I could see the whole ceiling, including the chandelier. However I couldn't see myself there. I was unsuccessfully trying to peer into the reflection but no one was there. I looked at the left corner of the room. There were three large plush chairs near a fireplace. Two of them were occupied by ballerinas. The first ballerina was a white swan, and the second one was a black swan. The third chair was empty. The ballerinas were talking and laughing. Flames from the fireplace were overhearing their conversation and were laughing with them. I walked further into the depths of the room. I walked across a long corridor. There were scenic pictures. They were from all over the world. There were drawings of lush green forests, snow-capped mountains and sand storms in desert. Also there were portraits of unfamiliar people from different epochs. After I found myself in usual nightclub. So I thought at first. Having looked closely, I noticed that people were dancing strangely. All of them were dancing like robots. I was scared by this spectacle.

- Would you like a drink? - a voice asked me.

I turned around. Behind me, there was a typical guy in a colored T-shirt, jeans and sneakers. He was holding a glass of a red bubbling liquid.

- What is it? - I was curious.

- It is hot cranberry juice. Don't you feel cold? - he asked.

Just at that moment, I felt very cold. I hugged myself and ran up the stairs. The next moment, I was in a small passage room. The right wall was hung with golden curtains. They were radiating warmth. I touched the fabric carefully. The curtain was so hot. I was pushing back the curtain and instantly stopped. I was astonished. The sand was pouring in at my feet. I saw an African landscape in front of me. It was hot. Giraffes were walking on hot sand under the high blue sky. I could feel the heat on my face. It was incredible. I wanted to make a step forward but I stopped. I drew the curtains and came back to shadow.

The library was the next room. There were books everywhere. They lay on the shelves, on the table and the sofa. Some of them were stacked on the floor. - Hello. Can I help you? - a girl with curly purple hair asked me.

- I ... I do not know - I said.

- Would you like a cup of coffee? - she asked.

- Yes, thank you - I said.

I was amazed. It was a strange magnetic and, at the same time, grim place.

- You can sit there – the girl pointed to a table near the window.

A man was sitting at the table. I went to the table across the room.

- Can I sit here? - I asked.

- Yes, please - he said smilingly.

He was tall and good looking. He was wearing a dark blue suit. There was a pocket on his chest with a red pocket square stripped with gold. Embroidered letter «J» was on the pocket square.

There was a quiet street with lots of lights outside the window.

- Where an I? - I asked.

- It is a garden! - he said.

- Actually, is it a library or .... - I was surprised by a sudden pause, while I looked through the window.

Outside the window was an old apple orchard.

- Here you go, your apple - the girl with purple hair said.

A baked apple laid in front of me on a plate. I took a spoon and tasted the flesh of the apple. It was very tasty. Sweet smell of baked apples with cinnamon rejoiced me. I stopped being afraid. I began to wonder what I would see next. For some time I was speaking with Mr. J. He is a fascinating companion. He told me many stories about himself.

If you let me, I'll tell you about them next time dear reader.

I thanked Mr. J, and continued my way.

Next I was on a bridge. Small boats were floating above water. There were people in large heavy dresses in those boats. They looked like characters from the 18th century. The women wore a lot of jewelry, and the men had swords. Two of them were trying to fight from different boats. A girl with long red hair was laughing over them.

- Sorry, but we are closed - the man who I met first said it.

He carried me down to the exit.

I became aware only when I was in taxi on my way. I was drawing a crown on the steamy window and trying to understand what I saw.

It definitely was the most crazy night of my life. Or it could be.
