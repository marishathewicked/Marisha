The blue-eyed land

Quiet peaceful people live in a blue-eyed land.
Since childhood, they know the taste of snow.
They are patient and friendly, they smile at the sun.
They scream "Au!" in the forest.
They sing songs around the campfire.
They are making crowns with flowers,
And let them float looking for love through black waters at Ivan Kupala's night.
Gloomy, they live in the gray autumn cities.
They rush to the subway every morning.
They love the expanse and the large scale.
They raise up ice palaces and build fountains.
They fly over the city with Marc Chagall.
They are riding with the wild Hunt of King Stach.
They dream. They love. They live.
They believe that a witch lives somewhere deep in the forest,
With her green-eyed cat who is walking on the creaking floor.
They believe that mermaids sing in the misty lake.
They believe that any wishes come true when the chiming clock is rising.
